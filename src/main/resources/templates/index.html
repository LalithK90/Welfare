Intro

AVSEC Welfare Society is a one of the large associations based on Civil Airports in Sri Lanka. AVSEC Welfare Society was formed on 23rd May 2008 by government with the aim of providing welfare facilities to serving all members and for their families in order to uplift their welfare function and ensure a smooth, continuous and regular functioning of the current welfare projects and to launch more innovative welfare projects for the benefit of all ranks, serving in the Aviation Security Division of AASL..

For welfare function they have do various types of transactions, request, grievances and etc. But those all functions are done by manually. They have to manage lot of files and books for their day to day activities. Because of that they have to face lot of problems. Following problems have been occurred in existing process not only that because of manual system, they have to update large number of contribution collection of records day-to-day. when considering about this tedious task, data redundancy and inconsistency between members has become the main problem in their manual system.
